Hello and welcome.

Chennai Ruby User Group is a community of Ruby enthusiasts in and around Chennai. We meet at least once monthly, and conduct workshops sometimes.

We have a Meetup page where we make meetup announcements, use Slack for chat, have a mailing list and we are on Twitter too.

We abide by our code of conduct.
